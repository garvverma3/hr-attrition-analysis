Key Findings

Critical insights discovered during the exploratory analysis phase.

Sales Department Vulnerability

The Sales department exhibits the highest attrition rate across the organization at 20.6%, significantly above the 16.1% average.

The Overtime Burnout

Employees working overtime are 3x more likely to leave (30% attrition) compared to those who do not (10% attrition).

Year 0 Flight Risk

Employees in their first year (Tenure: 0) face a massive 36% attrition rate, indicating onboarding is the biggest organizational risk.

The High Earner Cushion

High earners identified as outliers showcase extremely strong retention, with an attrition rate of only 4.4%.

Top Attrition Drivers Summary

The statistical analysis pinpoints four primary drivers of turnover: OverTime, Low Tenure, Low Job Level, and Low Monthly Income.
